# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

## Before Day One (HR Completes)

- [ ] Offer letter signed and on file
- [ ] Background check cleared
- [ ] IT notified — laptop and accounts to be ready by start date
- [ ] Badge access provisioned
- [ ] First-day schedule sent to new hire

---

## Day One

**Morning**
- [ ] Receive laptop from IT — confirm login works
- [ ] Receive ID badge — confirm building access
- [ ] Meet with Jordan Ellis: HR paperwork review (30 min)
- [ ] Complete I-9 verification — bring valid ID documents
- [ ] Sign and return: Confidentiality Agreement, Acceptable Use Policy, Employee Handbook acknowledgment

**Afternoon**
- [ ] Meet your manager — office tour and introductions
- [ ] Confirm Slack account is active; join team channels
- [ ] Confirm Microsoft 365 email and calendar are accessible
- [ ] Read the Welcome Document (provided in your welcome kit)

---

## Days Two and Three

**IT and System Access**
- [ ] HR Portal access confirmed — complete profile, verify payroll information
- [ ] Benefits enrollment started (deadline: within 30 days of start date)
  - [ ] Health insurance selection
  - [ ] Dental and vision selection
  - [ ] 401(k) enrollment (if applicable)
- [ ] Role-specific system access provisioned (your manager owns this)
- [ ] VPN access configured (if applicable)
- [ ] Any additional tool accounts set up (managed by manager)

**Team Integration**
- [ ] Attend team standup or team meeting (first scheduled occurrence)
- [ ] 1-on-1 scheduled with manager (if not already done)
- [ ] Introduction posted in company Slack #introductions channel

---

## Week One (Days Four and Five)

- [ ] 1-on-1 with manager: 30/60/90-day goals discussed and documented
- [ ] Role-specific onboarding activities started (per manager's onboarding plan)
- [ ] Emergency contact information submitted to HR
- [ ] Parking/commute details confirmed with building operations (if applicable)
- [ ] Any outstanding Day 1–3 items resolved

---

## Weeks Two and Three

- [ ] Benefits enrollment **completed** (confirm before Day 30 deadline)
- [ ] All required compliance training modules completed
  - [ ] Information security awareness (mandatory)
  - [ ] Anti-harassment training (mandatory)
  - [ ] Any role-specific required training (per manager)
- [ ] 30-day check-in scheduled with HR (Jordan Ellis will reach out)
- [ ] Any access or tool issues escalated and resolved
