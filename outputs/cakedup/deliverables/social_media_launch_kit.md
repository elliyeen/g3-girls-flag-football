## DM Reply Templates

When someone DMs asking about orders:

```
Hey! 🎂 You can pre-order through the link in my bio — takes
less than 2 minutes! Order by 8pm tonight for tomorrow's pickup.
Let me know if you have any questions 🩷
```

When confirming a received order:

```
Got your order! 🎉 Total is $[X] — send to:
Cash App: $[YourHandle]
Venmo: @[YourHandle]

I'll have your cakes ready for [pickup date] 🎂🩷
```

When a date is full:

```
Aw thank you for ordering! That date is full but I have
[next date] available — does that work? 🎂
```
