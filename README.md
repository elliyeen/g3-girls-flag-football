## About

Girls flag football is the fastest-growing youth sport in America. Arizona has no dominant girls-first flag football brand — yet.

**G3 Girls Flag Football (Gridiron Griots Girls)** is being built from the ground up in Arizona as a girls-first organization with a clear path to national scale and a Nike partnership. Starting in AZ, going national by 2027.

---

## The Mission

Give every girl in Arizona the chance to play organized football — and show the world what they can do.

- **Title IX alignment** — expanding girls' access to organized sports
- **NFL Flag affiliation** — legitimate, governed, recognized
- **Nike partnership pipeline** — built for it from Day 1
- **School district partnerships** — reaching girls where they already are

**Head Coach:** Hamza Abdullah — 7-Year NFL Safety (Denver Broncos · Arizona Cardinals · Cleveland Browns) · Founder, Pro Big Bros
