# G3 Girls Flag Football

**Arizona's girls-first flag football program — building athletes, confidence, and champions.**

🌐 **Live Site:** [elliyeen.github.io/g3-girls-flag-football](https://elliyeen.github.io/g3-girls-flag-football)

---

## About

Girls flag football is the fastest-growing youth sport in America. Arizona has no dominant girls-first flag football brand — yet.

**G3 Girls Flag Football** is being built from the ground up in Arizona as a girls-first organization with a clear path to national scale and a Nike partnership. We're starting in AZ, going national by 2027.

---

## The Mission

Give every girl in Arizona the chance to play organized football — and show the world what they can do.

- **Title IX alignment** — expanding girls' access to organized sports
- **NFL Flag affiliation** — legitimate, governed, recognized
- **Nike partnership pipeline** — built for it from Day 1
- **School district partnerships** — reaching girls where they already are

## Nike Partnership Strategy

G3 is built to attract Nike — not as a hopeful pitch, but as a prepared case:

| Nike Priority | G3 Alignment |
|---|---|
| Girls in Sport | Girls-only program — exact match |
| Underserved communities | School district partnerships in underserved AZ zip codes |
| Emerging sports growth | Flag football is the fastest-growing youth sport in the US |
| NFL partnership halo | NFL Flag affiliation + AZ Cardinals co-branding |
| Title IX advocacy | Directly expands girls' access to organized football |
| National scalability | AZ → TX → CA → FL |

**Realistic Year 1 deal:** $25K–$75K in product. **Year 2–3:** $250K–$500K+ national partnership.
