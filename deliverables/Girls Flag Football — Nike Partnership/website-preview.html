Why It Matters

Built Different,
By Design

Flag football is the fastest-growing youth sport in America. Girls deserve their own version of it — with coaching built around them, culture built around them, and a path to college built around them.

Athletic Development

Speed, agility, coordination, and strategy — flag football builds complete athletes at every age group, from first-time players to competitive seniors.

Teamwork & Leadership

Every player has a role. Every team has a voice. We build girls who lead on the field and carry that confidence into every part of their lives.

College Pipeline

Flag football is now sanctioned at the collegiate level. Our Senior division is designed to put players on the radar of college programs from Day 1.

Girls-First Culture

This is not a co-ed program with girls as an afterthought. Female coaches, female leadership, female-centered program design — every decision made for them.

G3 Training System

6 Stations.
One Complete
Athlete.

Every G3 camp and practice runs through our proven 6-station curriculum — built by NFL veterans and designed specifically for girls.

A
Station A
Foundation
Stance, alignment, and footwork. Every great player is built from the ground up. We start here — every single session.
B
Station B
Fundamentals
Blocking techniques, hand fighting, and conditioning. The tools every player needs before the playbook opens up.
C
Station C
Football
Route running, coverage, and game strategy. Real football IQ — reading defenses, creating separation, executing under pressure.
D
Station D
Focus
Mental reps, film study, and situational awareness. The game is 90% mental. We train the mind the same way we train the body.
E
Station E
Quarterback
Mechanics, footwork, reading the field, and leading under center. Every G3 player understands the game from behind center.
G
Station G
Finish
Live competition, game simulation, and championship mindset. We close every session the same way we close games — with everything on the line.
Head Coach

Coached by
The NFL.

NFL Veteran · 7 Seasons
Hamza Abdullah
Head Coach · Founder, Pro Big Bros
Denver Broncos AZ Cardinals Cleveland Browns

Hamza Abdullah spent seven seasons as a safety in the NFL — playing for the Denver Broncos, Cleveland Browns, and Arizona Cardinals. A Washington State University team captain, he brought the same discipline and leadership to every locker room he entered.

After football, Hamza founded Pro Big Bros — a mentoring organization that serves as professional big brothers to college and professional athletes. His mission: make sure the next generation of athletes is prepared for everything the game doesn't teach you.

"The field is where we build athletes. Everything else is where we build people."

Hamza brings NFL-level standards, genuine mentorship, and a deep commitment to youth development to every player who steps onto this field.
