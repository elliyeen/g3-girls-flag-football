---

## Business Sub-categories

| Sub-category | Description |
|---|---|
| Employee Wellbeing & Mental Health | Programs, support, and culture that protect staff mental health |
| Workplace Safety | Physical safety, OSHA / HSE compliance, incident response |
| Benefits & Healthcare | Health insurance, dental, vision, retirement, leave policies |
| DEI & Belonging | Diversity, equity, inclusion — who is welcome and how they thrive |
| Organizational Health | Culture surveys, engagement scores, org-level health metrics |
| Work-Life Balance | Flexible work, PTO policy, workload management |
| Employee Assistance Programs | Counselling, crisis support, financial wellness services |
| Occupational Health | Role-specific health requirements, return-to-work programs |
| Student & Learner Wellbeing | For edu orgs — student mental health, pastoral care, support services |

---

## Fits Across Org Types

| Org Type | Example |
|---|---|
| Sole Trader | Personal health as the only resource — protecting the operator |
| Small Business | Staff safety, basic benefits, keeping a small team healthy |
| Startup | Burnout prevention, founder mental health, early benefits design |
| SME | Formal safety program, first DEI initiatives, EAP introduction |
| Enterprise | Full occupational health dept, DEI strategy, global safety standards |
| Government | Civil service wellbeing programs, duty of care obligations |
| University | Student counselling services, faculty wellbeing, campus safety |
| K-12 School | Safeguarding, SEND support, staff wellbeing programs |
| Nonprofit / NGO | Volunteer safety, staff burnout in mission-driven work |
| Healthcare | Clinician wellness, shift safety, patient-facing staff support |

---

## Orchestrator Routing

Requests classified as **1 WELLBEING** route to:
- Employee programs → People Operations (6 WORK)
- Safety incidents → Risk Agent
- DEI initiatives → BA Agent (requirements first)
- Health benefits design → Finance Agent (5 MONEY) + Operations
